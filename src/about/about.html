About us

Export Mechanical Tool Join Stock Company (EMTC) orginally is state owner, established in 1960 and belong to the Machine and Industrial Equipment Corporation Ministry of Industry - Vietnam. We have been Join Stock Company since 2001 with 100% capital owned by the shareholders who have been working for company. We have over 50 years of processing and developing. We always interested in technical management and technology improvement, improving product quality, satisfying all foreign and domestic customers' requirement in conforming with the motto "Product quality is the survival condition and the decisive factor for the company's existence and subtainable prosperity".

The company is located in an area of 48.000m2 at address : Lot 15a, Quang Minh Industrial Zone, Me Linh District, Hanoi. We have been invested variety of machinery and equipments : Forging machine, Hammer machine, Upseting Machine, CNC Machining, Heat-treatment line, Surface treatment line and other advanced international assembly lines meeting international quality standards. Our Company has been certified ISO 9001:2008 , ISO 14001:2004, BSCI and be a member of FSC.

Company traditional products are handtools, spare part for Motorbike & Automobile such as : HONDA, YAMAHA, VMEP, SUZUKI, PIAGGIO, TOYOTA, FORD and some medical equipment, specialized machines, mechanical products, barbecue, kitchen tool set, housewares. A large amount of EMTC' product have been exported to Europe Market, Asian Market. Variety of company' product won golden medal at High Quality Industrial Trade Fair.
